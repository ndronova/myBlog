About [Me]

My name is Natalia.

I studied in the University of Queensland, doing Master in Business and majoring in Information Systems (basically, it is 70% business and 30% IT).

Before coming to Australia I made a promise to myself to say "Yes" to every opportunity that comes my way and to chase those opportunities that try sneaking by. So far it has paid off tremendously: I've volunteered as a teacher in Indonesia, participated in debating competitions all over Russia, started tutoring in UQ, took on an executive role in a student club and, what's more, I made personal growth a priority.

Through a lot of failures and successes I discovered my love of solving problems with technology. Since then I've been teaching myself front-end, and have just moved to Python and Java.

One day, I'd want this blog to be a place people will go for answers. Now, this is a place to learn, reflect, fail and build the expertise needed for the future.
